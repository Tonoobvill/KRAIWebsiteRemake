Applied Cyber Security

This program offers a sequence of courses designed to prepare students to further their education and career in the Information Technology career cluster. The content includes but is not limited to foundational knowledge and skills in computer and network security, security vulnerabilities, attack mechanisms and techniques, intrusion detection and prevention, cryptographic systems, system hardening, risk identification, incidence response, penetration testing, key management, access control, and recovery.
